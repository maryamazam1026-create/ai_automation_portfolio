# Day 2 – AI Note Summarizer

## Overview

This workflow automatically reads notes from Google Sheets, sends them to an AI model for summarization, and writes the summary back to the sheet.

## Workflow

Schedule Trigger
↓
Google Sheets (Read Rows)
↓
AI Model (OpenRouter)
↓
Google Sheets (Update Row)

## Tools Used

- n8n
- Google Sheets
- OpenRouter
- AI Language Model

## Business Use Case

A content creator can store rough ideas in Google Sheets and automatically receive clean summaries without manual editing.

- Google Sheets integration
